## Overview

Y Combinator (YC) is the startup accelerator that funds and advises early-stage companies. Under president [[Garry Tan]], YC has become one of the most visible institutional voices on AI-first engineering and company building.

## Key Ideas

- **Main Function** is YC's founder-focused video content series where Tan and others unpack patterns they're seeing across the portfolio
- YC's thesis on the current AI era centers on **"20x companies"** — tiny teams beating much larger incumbents through pervasive internal automation ([[The New Way To Build A Startup]])
- Tan's personal agent system runs inside YC's day-to-day operations (40+ skills, 25,000 files, 200 inputs/day) and is the source of his "thin harness, fat skills" architectural pattern
- Portfolio companies cited as 20x exemplars include Giga ML (voice agents, closed DoorDash with ~5 engineers), Legion Health (AI-native psychiatry, 4x growth with zero net new hires), and Phase Shift (12-person AR automation company)
