## Overview

Peter Pang is CTO of [[CREAO]]. He holds a PhD in physics from Stony Brook University. Previously a research scientist on Meta's Llama foundation team and worked at Apple on multimodal models.

## Key Ideas

- Led CREAO's transformation to AI-first engineering, restructuring the entire product architecture and engineering workflow from the ground up.
- Advocates that the ability to criticize AI is more valuable than the ability to produce code — credits his physics PhD for teaching him to question assumptions and stress-test arguments.
- Went from spending 60% of his time managing people to below 10%, shifting from managing to building (coding 9 AM to 3 AM most days).
- Believes one-person companies will become common as one architect with agents can do the work of 100 people.
- Advises engineers to train critical thinking, learn to evaluate arguments, and develop product sense/taste.
